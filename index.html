Who Am I?

I’m Caleb Lyons, I do brand design work, but that's not my only hat. I’ve found success in many areas of Design, UI/UX, packaging, ad campaigns, illustrative posters, and even some 3D designs. But why even offer those skills if you’re only going to get hired to do one of them? Well, you’re never going to just have website, or just packaging, or just an ad; no, you’re going to have a system working together and I think it’s very important to understand how each part works and interacts with the other whether you’re building that system or designing a part of it. My focus lies in those systems I like to try to understand how they push and pull each other, so that we can design something that doesn’t fight with itself, but fights for itself.
